Deflecting Pendulum

Description
Right Step-Through Front Kick

  1. Push Drag Reverse toward 7:30 into a Right Neutral Bow stance

    RH - Execute Downward Block to outside of opponent's leg

    LH - Checks at right shoulder

  2. Push Drag Forward toward 2:30

    LH - Execute Pressing Check to opponent's right arm

  3. RH - Execute Inward Elbow to opponent's face or humerous

  4. RH - Execute Outward Handsword to left side of opponent's neck

  5. RH - Execute Inward Elbow to opponent's face
